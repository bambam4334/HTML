Song Yuqi Of (G)-IDLE

Song Yuqi (Chinese: 宋雨琦, Korean: 송우기; born September 23, 1999), commonly known simply as Yuqi, is a Chinese singer and television host. She is part of the South Korean girl group (G)I-dle, which debuted in 2018. Since 2019, she has been co-hosting the Chinese TV show Keep Running.
